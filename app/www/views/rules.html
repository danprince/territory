Territory is a multi player strategy game that involves trying to own the largest territory on the board.

You can expand your territory by duplicating your tiles into neighbouring squares.

Each turn you start with the same number of moves as you have pieces on the board.

It costs 1 turn to move into an empy square.

It costs 3 turns to move into an enemy square.

The game ends when the board is full, or when all but one of the players are stuck.

The winner is the player with the highest score.
